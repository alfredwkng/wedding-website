Annie and Alfred's wedding celebration

We eloped on October 10, 2025, and are excited to celebrate our marriage with our closest friends and family on August 15 at Radio Star in Brooklyn.

Details

Venue

Radio Star 6 p.m. to 10 p.m.

13 Greenpoint Avenue, Brooklyn, New York

Radio Star is right at Transmitter Park, the former home of WNYC from 1937 to 1990. It hosts a beautiful view of the New York City skyline.

Getting There

It is a short walk from the Greenpoint Avenue stop on the G Train through the NYC Subway. It is also accessible by the NYC Ferry on the East River (B) route off the Greenpoint stop. For rideshare pick-up and drop-off, please set the location as the corner of West Street and Greenpoint Avenue.

On your way in, follow the signage and use the gated entrance to the left of the restaurant.

Dress Code

Semi-Formal / Cocktail

It'll be August in New York at a partially outdoor venue.

Maxi or midi dresses, suits and slacks, please no sneakers or jeans.

Here's an album of outfits we would wear ourselves if we could. (cool hats encouraged)

Schedule

Cocktail hour will be at Radio Star's outdoor patio from 6 p.m. to 7 p.m., followed by dinner inside the Neptune Room from 7 p.m. to 9 p.m.

A medley of Mediterranean dishes will be served during this time along with an open bar. There is no assigned seating and food is served buffet style.

Eat, drink, be merry, we married.

Accomodations

We want to make sure all our guests are comfortable and able to have a good time. Please fill out this form to let us know if you have any dietary restrictions or other requirements.

For parents, there is no dedicated mothers' room on-site, but there will be a small space available.
